The BATCH RECORD records the key manufacturing data for each tablet batch made at PharmaBusiness. The dissolution result (ie how long it takes for the tablets dissolves) is the key output based on the various manufacturing inputs. The dissolution results are monitored to communicate the state of control and performance (Product Health) of PharmaBusiness products.
